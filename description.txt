Adds many cool and silly guns to the game.

This mod adds:
-97 ranged weapons
-9 ammo types
-6 potions/buffs
-1 armor set
-14 accessories 
-6 material items

My first mod made/published, so don't be surprised if there is anything broken or unoptimized.
I had fun making this, and I hope you have fun playing with it.

NOTES AND DISCLAMERS:
This mod's weapons may or may not have issues with other mods that affect weaponry.
Some weapons are inspired either from other mods, videogames, or other sources.
